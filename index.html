How to use these tools
1
Enter account signals — IT Glue tech count, RMM agents if known, current K365 seats, and your bundle rate.
2
Review ARR potential — the full expansion opportunity is always visible. This is the target regardless of relationship status.
3
Work the waterfall — tick off actions stage by stage. Status moves from red to amber to green as you progress.
4
Export the plan — use the export button to generate a Word doc account plan to bring into your 1-to-1 or QBR.
